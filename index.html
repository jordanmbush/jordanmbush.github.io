About Me

I am Jordan Bush, and I am a Web Developer who has been programming for about 4 years. I started programming by teaching myself Visual Basic for Applications, and then studied at Phoenix College where I used C# in .NET building WPF applications. JavaScript is currently my strongest language. My most recent work consists of building React full stack applications.

I've had a passion for computer's since a young age. I've wanted to be a software developer since before I KNEW that I wanted to be a software developer and am extremely proud to have accomplished one of my childhood dreams.

I love learning new technologies, and wish I knew them all. My strengths are algorithms, problem solving, and my ability to get along with others.

This site was designed by myself, with some criticism from my beautiful wife.
